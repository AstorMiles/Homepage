ULTRAMARATHON - 100KM

TIME OF THE START

8:00 am, 2022, 31st of July

START/FINISH

Zhirmunai beach (Trail by Neris riverside)

TRACK

We start at Zhirmunai beach, then run by right side of the Neris' river, cross the bridge at Zverynas into the Vingis park and then continue by right side of the forest by Neris. Past the stadium we ascend the hill and continue around it returning to Zverynas bridge and continuing back to Zhirmunai beach. One course is approximately 20 km, to be repeated 4 times


NOTES

We recommend to bring someone to take care of You during the run
